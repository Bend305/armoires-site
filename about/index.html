About armoires.

Building the operating system for personal style.

An AI-powered wardrobe app, built by two engineers who got tired of staring at full closets and feeling like they had nothing to wear.

A company built around one stubborn problem.

The average closet holds 150+ pieces. Most people wear 20% of them. The rest sit unworn — not because they're not loved, but because there's no system to surface them at the right moment.

armoires is the platform that fixes that. We combine computer vision, multimodal AI, and personalized styling to turn every wardrobe into a working system — and the way you shop into something intentional rather than accidental.

We're bootstrapped, building in public beta, and proudly based in Denver.
